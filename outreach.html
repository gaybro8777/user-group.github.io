Publicity & Outreach

  • Research Presentations at ICSE 2019

    Ann Barcomb presented two papers at ICSE 2019 in Montreal, Canada, that she wrote as first author, and which are part of her PhD dissertation which she successfully defended in May 2019. Ann was supervised by Prof Brian Fitzgerald, Prof Dirk Riehle, and Dr Klaas-Jan Stol. The first paper featured in the Technical Track, the second was a Journal-First presentation of our TSE paper (in press). Klaas-Jan Stol also presented the paper "The ABC of Software Engineering Research" as a Journal-First presentation. Daniel Russo presented the paper "Soft theory: a pragmatic alternative to conduct quantitative empirical studies" at the CESSER-IP workshop.

  • Company Seminar on InnerSource

    In December 2018, Klaas-Jan Stol and Mario Schaarschmidt presented their paper "Company Soldiers or Gone-Natives: Role conflict and career ambition among firm-employed open source developers" at the international Conference on Information Systems (ICIS) which was held in San Francisco, CA, USA. This paper is the result of a collaboration with Dr Schaarschmidt who visited the lab in Spring 2018.

  • Book Launch: Adopting InnerSource: Principles and Case Studies

    Danese Cooper and Klaas-Jan Stol launched their book "Adopting InnerSource: Principles and Case Studies" at OSCON in July 2018. The book is published by O'Reilly, and was sponsored by PayPal.   • Panel at Grace Hopper Celebration

    Klaas-Jan Stol participated in a panel at the Grace Hopper Celebration 2017, which was held in Orlando, Florida, USA.
