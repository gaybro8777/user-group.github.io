Delphi Study: Managing InnerSource

About this Study

We are two researchers with University College Cork and Lero, the Irish Software Research Centre. We are studying management challenges in InnerSource. For this research, we are conducting an expert panel study, also known as a “Delphi” study. The expert panel will consist of 25 panelists who have expertise and experience in InnerSource, in a variety of roles. We’re contacting you to invite you to participate in the Delphi study. Participation would require a total time commitment of approximately 3 times 1 hour over the coming few months. The study is conducted in three rounds, and each round takes about 1 hour within a time window of about 2 weeks. Participation is anonymous. The overall process is shown in the figure below. In Round 1, we will ask you to provide input on our first prompt, namely, to identify challenges that you have encountered (now or in the past) in the management of InnerSource projects. Aggregated results will be fed back into Round 2, and so on.
